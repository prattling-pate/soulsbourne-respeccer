Weapon Properties

Input damage scaling (letter grades), stat requirements to wield them and their base damages (physical and magical) to find the optimal stats to maximise damage output.

For damage scaling, input the letter grade (S, A, B, C, D, E) or "-" if the weapon does not scale with that stat.

For stat requirements, input the minimum stat required to wield the weapon.

Note that all number values must be non-negative integers.
